      One Day Course on Fundamentals of Market Structure (from the New York Institute of Finance): a comprehensive study on the impact of technology on financial market systems

      There were 3 important lessons in this one day course


      • Introduction to Market Structure

      • Introduction to Market Structure Science

      • Fundamentals of Market Mechanics

      What We Learned in this one day course: We can now:

      • Recognize markets in general

      • Recognize the 2 dimensions of market systems

      • Identify the differences between Over The Counter (OTC) and Exchange Systems

      • Recognize how to define market participants

      • Explain the concept of liquidity

      • Identify the key concepts of financial market mechanics

      • Recognize how transaction frequency influences market evolution.
